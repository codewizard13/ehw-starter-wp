ABOUT THIS FOLDER:

ARCHITECTURE

Contains information architecture, visual sitemap, pages list, menus list, etc.

Usage:

Create a .TXT doc with https://tree.nathanfriend.io/ ASCII tree generator. This will render in Windows Explorer preview.

1) First, create a tab list of pages and subpages in the main top menu:

Home
About
	Our Ministry
	Our Leadership
	Our Beliefs
Blog
Shop
	Books
	Gifts
	Clearance
Contact

2) Use the nathanfriend link to generate this:

.
├── Home
├── About/
│   ├── Our Ministry
│   ├── Our Leadership
│   └── Our Beliefs
├── Blog
├── Shop/
│   ├── Books
│   ├── Gifts
│   └── Clearance
└── Contact

3) Copy the generated ascii tree into main-pages.txt or menu-top.txt, whichever makes more sense for the project.
